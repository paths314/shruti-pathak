8-bit-map is a website made by Shruti Pathak
for Currents: a-generative-web.

Nostalgia is intangible, timeless, and ever-changing all at once. As is the virtual space: video games, the internet; they are a map of our childhoods. How do we make sense of this world, if not by mapping it physically? This generative script creates dither maps, each representing 1 of 9 nostalgic video games.
